## RECITALS

**WHEREAS:**

A. VisioHealth is a proprietary healthcare technology company that has developed an AI-powered practice management platform incorporating sovereign data systems, machine learning, and intelligent automation tailored to the South African healthcare sector;

B. Dr. Lamola operates a medical practice and possesses clinical domain expertise, industry knowledge, and professional networks of value to the further development and market validation of VisioHealth's platform;

C. The Parties wish to enter into a strategic partnership encompassing both a commercial subscription relationship and a potential investment and intellectual property collaboration;

D. This Memorandum of Understanding (hereinafter **"MoU"**) sets out the Parties' mutual intentions and the framework within which definitive agreements will be negotiated and concluded;

E. The Parties acknowledge that this MoU, save for clauses 7, 8, 9, 10, 11, and 12, is not intended to create legally binding obligations, but rather to record the Parties' good faith intentions.

**NOW THEREFORE** the Parties record as follows:

---

## 1. DEFINITIONS

1.1. Unless the context indicates otherwise, capitalised terms used in this MoU shall have the meanings ascribed to them in the Mutual Non-Disclosure Agreement between the Parties dated [__________] (Reference: VH-NDA-2026-001), or as follows:

1.1.1. **"AI Agents"** means the artificial intelligence agents deployed within the Platform, including but not limited to triage agents, billing agents, intake agents, follow-up agents, and scheduler agents;

1.1.2. **"Definitive Agreements"** means the legally binding agreements to be negotiated and executed by the Parties, including but not limited to the IP Assignment and Protection Agreement, Service Level Agreement, and Commercial Agreement;

1.1.3. **"Exclusivity Area"** means the geographical area or medical specialty in which Dr. Lamola is granted exclusivity, as defined in clause 5;

1.1.4. **"Platform"** means the VisioHealth AI-powered healthcare practice management platform, including all software, APIs, AI models, dashboards, WhatsApp integrations, and related services;

1.1.5. **"Professional Plan"** means the subscription tier priced at R35,000.00 per month, the features of which are set out in Schedule A hereto;

1.1.6. **"WhatsApp Bot"** means the VisioHealth WhatsApp AI agent system integrated with the Platform for automated patient communication, appointment management, and clinical workflow support.

---

## 2. PURPOSE AND SCOPE OF PARTNERSHIP

2.1. The Parties intend to establish a multi-faceted partnership encompassing the following:

2.1.1. **Commercial Subscription:** Dr. Lamola's Practice shall subscribe to the VisioHealth Platform on the Professional Plan, subject to the terms of the Commercial Agreement and SLA;

2.1.2. **Clinical Advisory:** Dr. Lamola shall serve as a clinical advisor to VisioHealth, providing domain expertise, clinical workflow insights, and industry feedback to inform platform development;

2.1.3. **Investment Partnership:** Dr. Lamola may invest in VisioHealth on terms to be agreed in a separate shareholders' agreement or investment agreement;

2.1.4. **IP Collaboration:** The Parties shall collaborate on the development of healthcare-specific features, clinical protocols, and AI training data, with IP ownership governed by the IP Assignment and Protection Agreement;

2.1.5. **Market Validation:** Dr. Lamola's Practice shall serve as a reference site and case study (subject to patient confidentiality and POPIA compliance) for VisioHealth's marketing and sales efforts.

---

## 3. WHAT DR. LAMOLA RECEIVES

3.1. Subject to the execution of the Definitive Agreements, VisioHealth shall provide Dr. Lamola with:

3.1.1. **Professional Plan Platform Access** including:
   - Unlimited patient records management;
   - Up to ten (10) staff user accounts;
   - AI credits to the value of R5,000.00 per month;
   - Full suite of five (5) AI Agents (triage, billing, intake, follow-up, scheduler);
   - WhatsApp AI Bot with 24/7 automated patient communication;
   - POPIA-compliant data processing and storage;
   - ICD-10 SA automated coding assistance;
   - Medical aid claim processing and billing automation;
   - Daily automated data backups with 30-day retention;

3.1.2. **Lead Generation:** One hundred (100) qualified patient leads at onboarding, sourced through VisioHealth's proprietary lead generation systems;

3.1.3. **White-Label Branding:** Custom branding of the Platform with Dr. Lamola's practice logo, colour scheme, and custom domain name;

3.1.4. **Custom Domain:** A dedicated subdomain or custom domain (e.g., drlamola.visiohealth.co.za or a domain of Dr. Lamola's choosing);

3.1.5. **Dedicated Onboarding:** A dedicated account manager for the first thirty (30) days post-activation, including staff training, data migration, and workflow configuration;

3.1.6. **Priority Support:** Escalated support response times as set out in the SLA;

3.1.7. **Advisory Compensation:** [To be agreed -- options include equity participation, revenue share, reduced subscription fees, or a combination thereof];

3.1.8. **Exclusivity Protection:** Exclusivity within the Exclusivity Area as defined in clause 5.

---

## 4. WHAT VISIOHEALTH RETAINS

4.1. For the avoidance of doubt, VisioHealth shall at all times retain sole and exclusive ownership of:

4.1.1. the Platform in its entirety, including all source code, object code, software architecture, databases, and system designs;

4.1.2. all artificial intelligence models, including trained models, fine-tuned models, model weights, training methodologies, and inference pipelines;

4.1.3. all machine learning protocols, algorithms, and data processing methods;

4.1.4. the UIS Sovereign Data System architecture and all associated partnerships and licences;

4.1.5. the multi-tenant white-label practice management architecture;

4.1.6. all WhatsApp AI agent systems and integrations;

4.1.7. all AML protocols integrated into healthcare billing;

4.1.8. all ICD-10 SA coding automation methodologies;

4.1.9. the credit-based AI usage metering system;

4.1.10. the GP-to-specialist referral pipeline with AI triage;

4.1.11. all trademarks, trade names, branding, and goodwill associated with VisioHealth;

4.1.12. all customer data schemas, aggregated analytics, and platform usage data (excluding individually identifiable patient data, which belongs to the Practice);

4.1.13. all improvements, enhancements, derivative works, and modifications to the Platform, regardless of the source of the idea, suggestion, or feedback.

4.2. Dr. Lamola acknowledges that subscription to the Platform and participation in the partnership does not confer any ownership interest in the Platform or its underlying technology, save as may be expressly agreed in a separate investment or shareholders' agreement.

---

## 5. EXCLUSIVITY

5.1. Subject to the execution of the Definitive Agreements, VisioHealth shall grant Dr. Lamola exclusivity within the following parameters:

5.1.1. **Geographical Area:** [__________] (e.g., a specific suburb, town, or radius);

5.1.2. **Medical Specialty:** [__________] (e.g., general practice, ENT, dermatology);

5.1.3. **Duration:** The exclusivity shall apply for the duration of the commercial subscription, subject to the following conditions:

   (a) Dr. Lamola maintains an active Professional Plan subscription in good standing;

   (b) Dr. Lamola meets minimum usage thresholds as may be agreed from time to time;

   (c) VisioHealth reserves the right to review the exclusivity terms annually.

5.2. The exclusivity granted in clause 5.1 shall not apply to:

5.2.1. practices that were already subscribers to the Platform prior to the Commencement Date;

5.2.2. practices in different medical specialties within the same geographical area;

5.2.3. enterprise or hospital clients that operate across multiple geographical areas.

## 7. CONFIDENTIALITY

7.1. The Parties' obligations of confidentiality shall be governed by the Mutual Non-Disclosure Agreement between the Parties dated [__________] (Reference: VH-NDA-2026-001), which is incorporated herein by reference.

7.2. This clause 7 is legally binding on the Parties.

---

## 8. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

8.1. The ownership and treatment of intellectual property shall be governed by the IP Assignment and Protection Agreement between the Parties, which the Parties undertake to negotiate in good faith and execute within thirty (30) days of the date of this MoU.

8.2. Pending execution of the IP Assignment and Protection Agreement, the Parties agree that:

8.2.1. all Platform IP belongs exclusively to VisioHealth;

8.2.2. all clinical IP belonging to Dr. Lamola prior to the commencement of this partnership remains his exclusive property;

8.2.3. any IP created jointly shall be governed by the IP Assignment and Protection Agreement.

8.3. This clause 8 is legally binding on the Parties.

---

## 9. NON-SOLICITATION

9.1. During the term of this MoU and for a period of twelve (12) months following its termination, neither Party shall, directly or indirectly, solicit, entice, or recruit any employee, contractor, or consultant of the other Party.

9.2. This clause 9 is legally binding on the Parties.

---

## 10. DURATION AND TERMINATION

10.1. This MoU shall take effect on the date of signature by the last Party to sign and shall remain in force for a period of twelve (12) months, unless:

10.1.1. the Definitive Agreements are executed prior to the expiry of such period, in which case the relevant provisions of this MoU shall be superseded by the Definitive Agreements; or

10.1.2. either Party gives the other Party thirty (30) days' written notice of its intention to withdraw from the MoU.

10.2. Clauses 7, 8, 9, 11, and 12 shall survive the expiration or termination of this MoU.

---

## 11. GOVERNING LAW AND JURISDICTION

11.1. This MoU shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the Republic of South Africa.

11.2. The Parties submit to the non-exclusive jurisdiction of the High Court of South Africa, Gauteng Division, Pretoria.

11.3. This clause 11 is legally binding on the Parties.

---

## 12. GENERAL

12.1. **Good Faith.** The Parties undertake to negotiate the Definitive Agreements in good faith and to use their reasonable endeavours to conclude such agreements within the timeframes contemplated herein.

12.2. **No Assignment.** Neither Party may cede or assign its rights or obligations under this MoU without the prior written consent of the other Party.

12.3. **Counterparts.** This MoU may be executed in counterparts, including by electronic signature in accordance with the Electronic Communications and Transactions Act 25 of 2002.

12.4. **Costs.** Each Party shall bear its own costs in connection with this MoU.

12.5. This clause 12 is legally binding on the Parties.
