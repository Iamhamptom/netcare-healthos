## HPCSA COMPLIANCE FRAMEWORK

All strategies below operate within these boundaries:

| Allowed | Prohibited |
|---------|-----------|
| Factual practice information (name, location, qualifications, services) | Superlatives ("best ENT in Joburg") |
| Educational content about conditions | Patient testimonials or reviews on own platforms |
| Academic title and hospital affiliations | Before/after photos implying guaranteed outcomes |
| CPD presentations to colleagues | Canvassing (claiming superior knowledge/quality) |
| Directory listings with factual info | Touting (offering non-medical incentives) |
| Professional social media posts | Comparative claims against other practitioners |
| Website with services, conditions treated, contact info | Endorsement of products for financial gain |

**Key rule**: Practitioners may advertise services provided the advertising is "not unprofessional, untruthful, deceptive or misleading, or causes the public unwarranted anxiety." Educational content is the safest and most effective channel.

Sources:
- [HPCSA Advertising Guidelines](https://www.hpcsa-blogs.co.za/advertising-guidelines-for-practitioners/)
- [HPCSA Booklet 16 — Social Media Guidelines](https://www.hpcsa-blogs.co.za/wp-content/uploads/2019/09/HPCSA-Booklet-16-Ethical-Guidelines-on-Social-Media.pdf)
- [HPCSA Ethical Rules — Barter McKellar](https://www.bartermckellar.law/health-life-sciences-law-explained/understanding-the-ethical-rules-of-conduct-for-practitioners-registered-under-the-health-professions-act-1974)

### B. Pre-Authorisation Optimisation
**Common ENT procedures requiring pre-auth:**
| Procedure | NRPL Code | ICD-10 Codes | Tips |
|-----------|-----------|-------------|------|
| Tonsillectomy | 1101 | J35.1 (hypertrophy), J03.9 (acute tonsillitis), J35.0 (chronic) | Document frequency of episodes (7 in 1yr, 5/yr for 2yrs, or 3/yr for 3yrs) |
| Adenoidectomy | 1102 | J35.2 (adenoid hypertrophy), J35.3 (with tonsil hypertrophy) | Document obstruction severity, sleep study if available |
| Grommets (myringotomy + tubes) | 1071 | H65.2 (chronic serous otitis media), H65.3 (chronic mucoid) | Include audiogram showing conductive loss |
| FESS | 1221-1225 | J32.0-J32.9 (chronic sinusitis by site), J33.0 (nasal polyps) | Attach CT scan report, document failed medical Rx |
| Septoplasty | 1181 | J34.2 (deviated septum) | Document functional impairment, not cosmetic |
| Thyroidectomy | 1301/1302 | E04.1 (thyroid nodule), C73 (thyroid cancer) | FNA cytology result critical |

**Process** (Discovery as example):
1. Confirm date, hospital, tariff codes, ICD-10 codes with rooms
2. Call 0800 001 615 at least 48 hours before admission
3. Include all supporting documentation (audiograms, CT reports, scope findings)

- **Modifier use**: Correct use of bilateral modifiers, assistant surgeon codes
- **Pre-auth compliance**: Always pre-authorise — even "routine" cases get rejected without it
- **Follow-up**: Dedicated practice admin for claim tracking and appeals

### D. Preferred Provider Networks
