About Profmed
Profmed’s vision is to address the healthcare needs of South Africa’s
professionals. This is achieved through the tailored and
comprehensive design of benefits that suit the specific requirements
of professionals. With over 60 years’ experience, Profmed is one of
the longest established medical schemes in South Africa but we are
as relevant today as we were 60 years ago.
