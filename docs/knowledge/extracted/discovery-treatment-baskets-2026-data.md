# Discovery Treatment Baskets for CDL Conditions 2026

Extracted from: Discovery Health Medical Scheme Treatment Baskets for CDL Conditions 2026
Scheme Registration: 1125

## Key Rules
- Claims paid from CIB if condition approved on Chronic Illness Benefit
- Tests/consultations pro-rated based on date of approval
- 4 GP consultations per year (at nominated network GP, in full)
- Co-payment if non-network/non-nominated GP used (80% DHR)
- ICD-10 diagnosis codes required on all claims
- Diagnostic basket: tests paid in full at time of initial diagnosis
- Annual treatment basket: limited consultations and procedures per condition

## GP Consultation Rules by Plan
- Executive: 4 consults/year at Discovery Health GP Network (80% DHR if out-of-network)
- Comprehensive/Core/Priority/Saver: 4 consults/year at nominated primary care GP in network
- KeyCare: must use KeyCare network GP
- Smart/Smart Saver: consults from day-to-day benefits
