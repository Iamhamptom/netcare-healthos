## BUSINESS MODEL: Platform + Managed Care Hybrid

Netcare Primary Care doesn't employ most of its doctors. It's a **landlord + admin services platform** — they own the clinics, and 568 independent GPs/dentists practice from them, paying facility and management fees.

## FOUR REVENUE ENGINES

| Engine | How It Works | Who Pays |
|--------|-------------|----------|
| **Fee-for-service** | GPs bill medical schemes for consultations, procedures, pharmacy | Medical aid schemes |
| **Capitation (Prime Cure)** | Fixed per-member-per-month (PMPM) fee to manage 254,000 lives across 49 scheme options | Medical schemes |
| **Occupational health** | Corporate SLAs for medical surveillance, exec medicals, injury management (mining, automotive, finance, govt) | Employers |
| **Cash/self-pay** | Discounted GP consultation vouchers for uninsured patients | Patients directly |

## WHAT THEY OPERATE

| Facility Type | Count | What It Does |
|--------------|-------|-------------|
| Medicross clinics | 55 | GP, dental, pharmacy, optometry, radiology, physio, day surgery — across 8 provinces |
| Prime Cure centres | ~33 | Located in townships and industrial areas — capitated managed care for low-income populations |
| Retail pharmacies | 41 | Co-located in clinics |
| Day theatres | 12 | Minor surgical procedures |
| Occ health centres | 3 | Sandton, Durban, Cape Town |
| **TOTAL** | **88 clinics, 3.5M patients/yr** | |

## SCALE NUMBERS

- 568 independent doctors and dentists
- 10,000+ contracted providers in Prime Cure's network (3,877 doctors/dentists)
- 254,000 capitated lives (Prime Cure)
- ~3.5 million patient visits/year (combined)
- 8 of 9 provinces

## FINANCIALS (FY2025, year ended Sept 2025)

| Metric | FY2025 | FY2024 |
|--------|--------|--------|
| Revenue | R662M | R712M (-7%) |
| EBITDA | R162M | R164M |
| EBITDA margin | 24.5% | 23.0% |

The 7% revenue drop was caused by one large occupational health contract non-renewal (ended May 2025). Excluding that, underlying growth was +2.8% — barely keeping pace with inflation.

## KEY CHALLENGES (OUR OPPORTUNITY)

| Challenge | Detail | Our Product That Fixes It |
|-----------|--------|--------------------------|
| Flat real growth | 2.8% underlying growth vs ~5% inflation — shrinking in real terms | Claims Analyzer recovers lost revenue |
| Claims leakage | 568 independent GPs all coding differently, no standardization | Switching Engine + Claims Analyzer |
| Contract concentration risk | Lost one occ-health contract → 7% revenue swing | Diversified revenue via better patient retention (WhatsApp Router) |
| GP shortage | Can't attract/retain practitioners | HealthOps reduces admin burden per GP — makes Medicross more attractive |
