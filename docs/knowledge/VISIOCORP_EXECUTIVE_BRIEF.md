## The Problem

South Africa's R259.3 billion medical schemes claims market suffers from a systemic **15-20% rejection rate**. The consequences are severe:

- **R40 billion/year** paid out-of-pocket by members for denied claims
- **74% ICD-10 coding accuracy** — one in four diagnoses coded incorrectly
- **R22-28 billion/year** lost to fraud, waste, and abuse (50% is waste, not fraud)
- **R50-R200 per rejected claim** in administrative rework costs

The #1 cause of rejection — incorrect or missing ICD-10 codes (30% of all rejections) — is almost entirely preventable with the right technology.

Yet **no practice management software vendor in South Africa offers AI-powered claims intelligence**. Not Healthbridge. Not GoodX. Not Solumed. Not CGM. Not Altron.

### What Our AI Does

**Before submission**: Validates every claim against scheme-specific rules, ICD-10 specificity requirements, tariff code validity, modifier appropriateness, formulary compliance, and clinical guidelines.

**Predicts rejections**: 35 engineered ML features achieve **AUC 0.90-0.96** in hybrid rule-based + machine learning architecture.

**Auto-corrects errors**: Three-tier system (fully automated / semi-automated / human-required) fixes preventable errors before they become rejections.

**Identifies PMBs**: Automatically flags Prescribed Minimum Benefit conditions, ensuring practices capture R40B in underpaid member benefits. PMB appeals are won **69.4%** of the time — proof that members are being systematically denied benefits they are entitled to.
