## DISCOVERY'S GAP (PRIMARY PITCH ANGLE)

### What Discovery Has
- Vitality AI (Google Cloud): member-facing wellness, rewards, 2,800+ health dimensions
- HP Zone: basic portal, claims statements, limited booking
- DrConnect: telehealth
- HealthID: EHR for providers
- 50M+ life years of data

### What Discovery Doesn't Have
- **NO practice-level AI** — no AI helping GPs code correctly, predict rejections, or optimize billing
- **NO white-label platform** — practices can't brand the system
- **NO multi-tenant architecture** — can't serve multiple practice chains

### VisioCorp = Practice Layer Between Member and Claim
- Fills the gap between Discovery's member-facing AI and the claim submission
- Ensures clean claims BEFORE they hit FICO Blaze Advisor
- Reduces the 15-20% rejection rate that costs practices cash flow
