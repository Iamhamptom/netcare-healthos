| **openEHR** | No adoption in SA |

## GOVERNMENT & POLICY

### NHI Status (March 2026)
- Signed May 2024. **ConCourt hearing: 5-7 May 2026**. Implementation fully on hold.
- If implemented: Single payer, standardised ICD-10/NAPPI/FHIR, DRG/capitation models
- Full implementation: 2035 (estimated). Medical schemes → complementary cover only.

### CMS Industry Data (2024)
- 76 schemes, R230B gross contributions, R193B claims paid (84% claims ratio)
- Hospital claims: 38% of total (~R73B). Specialist: 18%. Medicines: 16%. GP: 10%.
- ~11,500 formal complaints/year. ~55% claims-related.

### National Digital Health Strategy
- FHIR R4 mandated for all new government health systems
- HPRS (Health Patient Registration System): 44M+ patients registered
- DHIS2: Backbone for all 52 health districts
- NHI IT systems being procured: Claims Management, Fraud Detection, Revenue Collection, HTA

### Budget (2024/25)
- Total health allocation: R259.2B. NHI conditional grant: R4.6B.
- Digital health budget: ~R1.5-2.0B (estimated across programs)
