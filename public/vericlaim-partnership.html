Why Now

Four reasons this
can't wait.

DRIVER 01

AngularJS End-of-Life

AngularJS reached end-of-life in December 2021. Every month on legacy tech increases security exposure and makes modernisation harder. The technical debt is compounding.

DRIVER 02

NHI Requires FHIR

The NHI Bill mandates FHIR interoperability for all healthcare systems. VeriClaim currently has zero FHIR capability. Health OS has a working R4 CapabilityStatement ready to extend.

DRIVER 03

Competitors Are Moving

GoodX and Healthbridge are actively adding AI features and analytics. Every quarter without an AI story risks losing practices to competitors who promise smarter tooling.

DRIVER 04

SpesNet Already Has ML

SpesNet Group deploys machine learning at funder level. The AI expertise exists within the group — it's time to bring that same intelligence to practice-level operations.
