## HOT TOPICS — What Content Gets Attention Right Now

### Top 7 Engagement Topics (LinkedIn + Media)

1. **PEPFAR funding cuts** — $460M/yr frozen, 8,000+ healthcare workers displaced, 150K-296K projected additional HIV infections. THE emotional story of 2025-2026.
2. **NHI reality check** — 9 court cases, Constitutional Court hearing May 2026, no effective dates proclaimed. Everyone confused, nobody explaining clearly.
3. **AI in healthcare** — 61% of SA healthcare leaders already using AI. No regulatory framework. Massive adoption-regulation gap.
4. **Healthcare worker crisis** — 18.6% specialist vacancy rate, 20,000 nurses unemployed while facilities understaffed. The paradox.
5. **NCD screening gap** — 91.1% hypertensives uncontrolled, 52-61% diabetics undiagnosed. Hidden epidemic.
6. **Medical aid affordability** — 41% cutting back, membership below 15%, NHI stalled. The "gap" population growing.
7. **POPIA health data regulations** — New 2025 amendments, health data as "Special Personal Information." Nobody creating practical guidance.
