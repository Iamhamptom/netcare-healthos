## 1. SERVICES

1.1 The Consultant shall provide healthcare practice optimisation consulting services, including:
- Billing and claims workflow analysis
- Revenue cycle optimisation
- Administrative process review and recommendations
- Staff training on best practices for claims processing, coding, and scheduling
- AI-assisted data analysis using the Consultant's proprietary tools

1.2 Services are advisory in nature. The Consultant does not access, store, or process the Client's patient records, electronic health systems, or personal information databases. All analysis is performed using the Consultant's own tools on anonymised or aggregated data presented during consultation sessions.

4.1 Following each engagement, the Consultant shall provide:

4.2 All deliverables are advisory. Implementation is at the Client's discretion and risk.

## 5. DATA AND CONFIDENTIALITY

5.1 The Consultant shall not request or require access to the Client's patient records, electronic health record systems, or any database containing personal information as defined in POPIA.

5.2 Where the Client chooses to share anonymised or aggregated data during a consultation session for analysis purposes, such data remains the Client's property and shall not be retained by the Consultant after the session.

5.3 Both parties shall treat all business information exchanged during the engagement as confidential, subject to the Mutual Non-Disclosure Agreement signed alongside this Agreement.

5.4 The Consultant uses proprietary AI-powered tools during consultation sessions. No Client data is uploaded to, stored in, or processed by these tools. The tools are used solely to demonstrate capabilities and provide real-time analysis on data the Client verbally presents or displays on their own screens.
