## 1. PURPOSE

The parties intend to exchange confidential information in connection with healthcare practice consulting services and wish to protect such information from unauthorised disclosure.

## 2. CONFIDENTIAL INFORMATION

2.1 "Confidential Information" means any information disclosed by one party ("Discloser") to the other ("Recipient"), whether orally, in writing, or by demonstration, that is:
- (a) marked or identified as confidential; or
- (b) by its nature would reasonably be considered confidential,

including but not limited to: business operations, financial information, pricing, client lists, practice workflows, methodologies, software, trade secrets, and business strategies.

2.2 Confidential Information does **not** include information that:
- (a) is or becomes publicly available through no fault of the Recipient;
- (b) was already known to the Recipient before disclosure;
- (c) is independently developed by the Recipient without reference to the Confidential Information;
- (d) is lawfully received from a third party without restriction.

## 3. OBLIGATIONS

3.1 The Recipient shall:
- (a) keep all Confidential Information strictly confidential;
- (b) not disclose it to any third party without the Discloser's prior written consent;
- (c) use it only for the purpose described in clause 1;
- (d) limit internal disclosure to employees and contractors who need to know and who are bound by equivalent confidentiality obligations;
- (e) take reasonable measures to protect the Confidential Information from unauthorised access or disclosure.

3.2 The Recipient may disclose Confidential Information if required by law, regulation, or court order, provided the Recipient:
- (a) notifies the Discloser in advance where legally permitted; and
- (b) limits disclosure to what is legally required.

## 4. NO LICENCE OR WARRANTY

4.1 No licence or rights to intellectual property are granted by this Agreement.

4.2 Confidential Information is provided "as is" without warranty.

## 5. RETURN OR DESTRUCTION

5.1 Upon written request or termination of discussions, the Recipient shall promptly return or destroy all Confidential Information and confirm destruction in writing.

## 6. TERM

6.1 This Agreement is effective from the date above and remains in force for **2 years**.

6.2 The obligations of confidentiality survive termination for a further **2 years** (4 years total from signing).

## 7. REMEDIES

7.1 The parties acknowledge that breach may cause irreparable harm not adequately compensated by damages, and the Discloser may seek injunctive relief in addition to any other remedies.

## 8. GENERAL

8.1 **Governing Law:** Laws of the Republic of South Africa.

8.2 **Jurisdiction:** High Court of South Africa, Gauteng Division.

8.3 **Entire Agreement:** This is the entire agreement regarding confidentiality and supersedes all prior discussions.

8.4 **Amendment:** Only by written agreement signed by both parties.

8.5 **Severability:** Invalid provisions do not affect the remainder.

8.6 **No Waiver:** Failure to enforce a right is not a waiver of that right.
