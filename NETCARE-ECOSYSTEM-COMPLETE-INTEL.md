## 3.1 CareOn (Electronic Medical Record System)
- **Netcare's flagship EMR** — largest and most transformative digital implementation
- **R82 million invested** in 2023 alone
- **7 years of development**
- **Deployed at 26+ hospitals** (full acute care rollout targeted)
- **34,000+ users** across 7 provinces
- **URL**: https://netcare.digital/

### Technology Partners:
| Partner | Role |
|---------|------|
| **Deutsche Telekom (Clinical Solutions)** | Electronic medical records platform |
| **Apple (iOS)** | Mobile operating platform — iPads for all clinical staff |
| **Capsule Technologies** | Digital integration of all medical equipment and devices |
| **IBM Watson Health Micromedex** | Drug interaction, dosage, safety information |
| **SAP** | Finance, administration, billing, procurement, materials management |

### Key Capabilities:
- Electronic documentation and management of all patient records
- Electronic ordering and retrieval — pathology labs, radiology, blood bank
- Mobile access — specialists, nurses, healthcare practitioners can access patient data from any location on iPad
- Drug safety: IBM Watson Micromedex checks all drug dosages, interactions, duplications, allergies across 1.8M+ in-hospital scripts/year
- Eliminates up to **60% of potential medication errors**
- **R60M saved** in stationery/printing costs
- **R12M saved** in physical storage costs
- **50-60% reduction** in medicolegal costs anticipated

### Notable:
- Netcare is the **single-largest buyer of iPads in the southern hemisphere** due to CareOn rollout
- Won international acclaim — South African healthcare system garnering international recognition

## 3.7 Corsano Health Wearables (NEW — 2025)
- **Strategic partnership** with Dutch company Corsano Health
- Continuous wearable monitoring for **all general ward patients**
- Phased rollout to **~6,000 beds nationally**
- Pilot at flagship Netcare facility first

### Device Capabilities:
- Heart rate (continuous)
- Respiratory rate
- Core body temperature
- Oxygen saturation (SpO2)
- **Cuffless blood pressure** (unique at this scale)
- Cardiac arrhythmias and atrial fibrillation detection
- Predictive algorithms and early-warning alerts

### Integration:
- Data streams to Corsano cloud platform
- Shared with Netcare CareOn medical records system
- Automated early warning scores
- Centralized monitoring and alerting dashboards
- Replaces manual periodic vital-sign readings by nurses
- Shifts care from **reactive to predictive model**

### Wards Covered:
- General wards
- Maternity wards
- Psychiatric wards
- Rehabilitation wards

# SECTION 10: COMPETITIVE INTELLIGENCE SUMMARY

## What Netcare Does Well:
- Massive physical footprint (49 hospitals, 55 clinics, 71 dialysis centres, 15 mental health facilities)
- CareOn EMR is genuinely world-class (Deutsche Telekom + Apple + IBM Watson)
- Wearable rollout (Corsano) puts them ahead of Mediclinic and Life Healthcare
- Strong brand recognition and 911 emergency services
- R26B+ revenue, profitable, listed on JSE

## Where the Gaps Are (YOUR OPPORTUNITY):
1. **Primary care digital experience is fragmented** — multiple booking systems, no unified patient journey
2. **No WhatsApp integration** — in a country where WhatsApp dominates
3. **No AI triage or chatbot** — patients must know what they need before booking
4. **No real-time queue/wait time visibility** — walk-in experience is blind
5. **No automated patient engagement** — reminders, follow-ups, chronic care nudges
6. **No patient-facing analytics** — no health dashboards, trends, or insights
7. **Clicks pharmacy outsourcing** means Netcare lost direct pharmacy engagement
8. **Chronic disease management** has no digital patient-facing layer
